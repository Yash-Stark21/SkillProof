## Product rules to protect

- A supported skill has qualifying repository evidence.
- Every usable claim links to qualifying evidence.
- Partial coverage never becomes confident absence.
- Job Fit and Portfolio Quality remain separate.
- Parsed job skills require explicit user confirmation.
